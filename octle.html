WHAT IS IT ABOUT?

 

OCTLE is a quick article about our precious life which we
think we owe for a long time, but the bitter truth is that
life is short and we must learn to live life to the fullest.

 

WE ARE HERE ONLY ONCE

 

This Article Covers:

  1. ...why we must live in the present.
  2. ...why we must respect each and every being on Earth.
  3. ...how not to regret our words and actions.
  4. ...how precious life is.
  5. ...and finally how easily it could be taken away.

LIVE IN THE PRESENT!

 

Like they say;"Yesterday's the past, tomorrow's the future,but today is a gift.   That's why it's called the present."

Human beings are more worried about the things of the past or the future rather than living
life like it goes.We are here in this world for only a short period of time and we don't even
get time to live it completely! That's why most of us die in a guilt of not fulfilling our
dreams.When we think about a bad incident that happened in the past,we are actually living
that mood twice!Why rather not create a pleasant memory now?Things of the past does not define
the way we are today....but what you do today is gonna make an impact on the way the world sees
you tomorrow.So erase those bad influences of the past, learn from your mistakes, cut off ties
with people you dont feel good to be around. This will polish you to face the future.
 
 

EVERYONE DESERVES TO BE RESPECTED.

 

"When we show our respect for other living things, they respond with respect for us."

  No one is born, nor raised to live a left out life from the society. Even if it is a poor vagabond, or a rich
businessman.Everyone deserves respect. Human behaviour is so strange that we believe we have developed a lot
from the past, but reality is just worse! We are forced to be in this judgemental society where people of one
kind is treated different from the others.It's so pathetic that we don't realise where we all come from! We
are all the same type of flowers in the same garden of love, happiness, brotherhood and peace.Remember the rule
of Karma...WHAT GOES AROUND,COMES AROUND.
 
 

“DON'T CRY OVER SPILLED MILK!”

 

"There is no room for regrets in this world if there are no rooms of differences."

  Have you been in a situation where you reacted in a manner you regretted? Probably yes! Right? It's ok...
we all learn from mistakes. We might have hurt someone with our words or actions, about which we spend sleepless
nights regretting! But once you understand the rule of the world, you are free from this guilt. We have two ears
and one mouth for a reason; to hear as twice as much as you talk! Our words must be chosen wisely 'cause
once anything is said,it can't be taken back!
 
 

L.I.F.E.

 

Love Is For Everyone

  Life is short. Life is precious!We are here for a purpose. We must fulfill it by loving everyone.We don't know how long
we will be alive for it's short and unpredictable.So as long as we are here, make sure you live it great.
Always Remember:
  • Life is a beautiful, miraculous gift that needs to be savored
  • Gratitude is the pathway to hope
  • There is a reason for every failure we experience in life
  • Pain makes us stronger and more aware
  • We’re here today, but we can be gone tomorrow so live life to the fullest
  • All of us are on unique journeys in life and we shouldn’t compare ourselves to others
  Remove all negativity and hate from your life, and forgive those around you for whatever it is they might have wronged you for.
You don’t need to forget. Just forgive. It will lighten the load and make you further appreciate the fragility of life.
 
 

LIFE IS SHORT.

 

Imagine the person you are ignoring for a while does not wake up tomorrow?Would you regret your pride?

 Before blasting out at someone, before critisizing someone, before hating someone for literally no reason, or before wishing bad
for someone because they were not being the way you expected them to be;STOP! What if that person is no more the
very next day? Can you go back in time to change things as it happened?NO! You will live in the haunting memory of that person.
So be good to all...and get the good from the world.You don't know what would happen to anyone the very next second! So keep this
bitter truth of life in mind DEATH.
